Lake Tuzla

This lake, which can be reached via a road branching off to the west from the Kayseri-Sivas highway, is located between the villages of Büyüktuzhisar, Palas, and Kermelik.

The lake dries up completely during the summer months, and the salt is collected by the local people.

Irrigated agriculture is practiced around the lake.
