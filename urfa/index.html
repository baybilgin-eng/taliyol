Urfa and Göbekli Tepe

Urfa, with its fish-filled lake and tombs of prophets, is a city of civilization whose history dates back thousands of years.

This is a city of civilization where the first known temple in history is located: Göbekli Tepe.

Traveling within Urfa is quite difficult; the roads are narrow and the drivers are impatient.

It gets so hot in the summer that even the air you breathe burns your insides.

After visiting the Balıklı Göl (Fish Lake) and the tombs of the prophets in Urfa, you should definitely also visit Göbekli Tepe.

Göbekli Tepe was discovered as a result of excavations carried out in an arid area outside the city, and its surroundings are completely empty.

Numerous rocks bearing religious engravings from tens of thousands of years ago have been systematically carved and arranged here.
