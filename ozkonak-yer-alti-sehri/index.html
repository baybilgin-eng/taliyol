Ozkonak Underground City

Located approximately 15 km from Avanos, this underground city consists of several rooms of varying sizes and a tunnel system connecting these rooms.

This city, which extends three levels below ground, contains areas such as stables, a kitchen, a winery, a food storage area, a ventilation tunnel, and keystone entrances.

This not-so-large underground city is worth seeing if it's on your way.
