Urgup Mineral Water Spring

Located east of the TOKİ housing complex in Ürgüp, this natural mineral water source has very salty water.

The water has not been analyzed in any way, so I recommend not drinking it.

Water emerges from several points and collects at several points in the form of small pools.

Gas is also observed escaping in the form of bubbles at the point where the water emerges.

The water temperature is around 15 degrees.

The area where it originates smells foul and is heavily polluted by people, which is why it is also referred to as "Boklu İçmece" (Filthy Spring) in some sources.
