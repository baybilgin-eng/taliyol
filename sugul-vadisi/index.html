Shugul Valley

Access to the Şuğul Valley, located within the borders of Gürün, is quite easy. Simply follow the sign on the road when entering Gürün from the west, and the road leads all the way to the valley's entrance. At the entrance, there's a trout farm; then, you can follow a stone-paved walking path into the deep valley. A stream flows through the middle of the valley, deepening in places and cascading down small waterfalls. The walking path is quite high above ground and protected by iron railings. After the walking path ends, picnic areas have been created, and the path continues, but from this point onwards, it is entirely unpaved.

Fish live in the stream flowing through the valley, and fishing is possible. Although entering the water is prohibited, it's possible to see children swimming. Şuğul Valley is a very suitable valley for trekking.
