Necropolis of Yeşilöz (Tağar) Village

It is a village in the Ürgüp district of Nevşehir province. In records from 1927, the village's name is recorded as Tağar (Greek: Theodor). The village has a very old and unique Greek Orthodox church dedicated to Saint Theodoros. Theodoros means "God Gave".

Along the rock face south of the village are rock-carved defensive structures and a necropolis.

The decorations on the facade of one of the tombs located within the necropolis are quite interesting.

In this area, a large amount of fresh water seeps from inside the rock, and the villagers have built a pool to store it.
