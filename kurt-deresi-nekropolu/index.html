Kurt Creek Necropolis

Located approximately 2 km west of Karlık village in Ürgüp district of Nevşehir province, the Kurt Deresi Necropolis is an interesting historical site with rock-carved tombs featuring human silhouettes on its walls.

In the eastern part of the region, there are remnants indicating the presence of a settlement.
