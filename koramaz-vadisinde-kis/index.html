Winter in Koramaz Valley

Koramaz Valley is adorned with flowers in spring, sweetened with fruits in summer, and turns yellow with leaves in autumn…

Did you think it would be idle in the winter?

Koramaz Valley is especially beautiful when covered in its white blanket of snow.

The cold of winter, the white of the snow, and the warm atmosphere of the historic valley create a sense of peace.
