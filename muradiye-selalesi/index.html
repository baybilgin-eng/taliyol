Muradiye Waterfall

Located within the borders of Muradiye district in Van province, the waterfall can be reached via the Muradiye road, which branches off from the Van-Erciş highway. Continue on the road without turning into Muradiye town center, and after a few kilometers, turn left at the Muradiye Waterfall sign. The waterfall flows particularly vigorously in the spring months, creating a journey that leads all the way to the alkaline waters of Lake Van. There is also a suspension bridge right next to the waterfall, and seating areas and gazebos are designed for picnics. This is a waterfall too beautiful to miss.
