Kocabaylar Kebab Restaurant

Located in the square called Bayram Yeri in the center of Denizli, Kocabaylar Kebap Salonu makes "Denizli Kebab".

When you hear the word kebab, don't immediately think of diced or minced meat, skewered, and cooked over charcoal.

Denizli Kebab is actually made from whole lamb meat that is cooked in the oven.

We wouldn't be wrong to describe the place as casual.

Lamb is served in a single plate in the middle of the table, regardless of the number of people, and no forks, knives, or spoons are provided.

They say this kebab is meant to be eaten by hand, and even if you insist on a fork, they'll say they don't have one.

They serve plenty of thin flatbread under, on top of, and next to the kebab, thoroughly coating it in lamb fat before serving.

They serve large slices of tomato, cucumber, and onion as a salad.

It tastes amazing, has a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture, I highly recommend trying it.

This place has already received five stars from Vedat Milor.

According to a newspaper report, this place is among the top 10 kebab restaurants in Türkiye.
