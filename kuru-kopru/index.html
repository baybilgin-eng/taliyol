Dry Bridge

Kuru Köprü is actually a Byzantine aqueduct and is located in the Kuruköprü neighborhood within the boundaries of Talas district.

Repair work is being carried out on the arch.

It's quite a long and high arch, situated over a now-dry stream.

The aqueduct, with its magnificent structure, has a captivating appearance and is definitely worth seeing.

In the past, the Salguma stream was channeled to the city center via this aqueduct.
