Subaşı Seljuk Cemetery

Located east of Subaşı (Üsgübü, İsgobi) village in Kayseri province, on the western slopes of the Koramaz mountain, this cemetery is currently in a dilapidated state. Only a few of the tombstones have survived to the present day, untouched by time and human activity. All the stones are broken and lying on the ground; they are a terracotta color and bear various inscriptions and designs. I believe that further excavation work in this area will uncover more graves and tombstones. Currently, the tombstones stand unprotected and neglected. I believe this cemetery, which I consider an important cultural and historical heritage for the region, should be properly restored and maintained; this is our duty to our history.
