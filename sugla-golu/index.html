Sugla Lake

It is located between the Seydişehir and Bozkır districts of Konya.

You can reach the lake via the road from Seydişehir to Bozkır, passing through the village of Yalıhüyük.

It is a wetland and home to numerous bird species.

Reaching the lake is quite difficult because there are no signs or roads; it can only be accessed via a gravel path along the irrigation canal.

Lake water is transported through canals for irrigation purposes, and agriculture is carried out on a very significant scale.

Today, the lake's name has been changed to an irrigation reservoir by the State Hydraulic Works (DSİ), and it sometimes even runs completely dry.

I observed many birds in the lake, and I've been told that fishing also takes place there.
