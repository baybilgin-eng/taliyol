Rock Churches of Ağırnas

There are a total of 9 rock churches located in the Koramaz Valley section of Ağırnas.

We believe these churches date back to the Byzantine period.

Church entrance doors are generally very carefully crafted.

The church has a double apse, and no traces of frescoes remain in either of them.
