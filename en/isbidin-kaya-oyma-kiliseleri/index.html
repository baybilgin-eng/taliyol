Isbidin Rock-Carved Churches

I previously mentioned a rock church located in Isbidin, which is notable for its magnificent frescoes.

In this article, I will discuss a total of six rock-carved churches in Isbidin, separate from the church mentioned above.

Of the churches pictured below, four are very close together, two are back-to-back, and one has two separate apses and altars.

Most of the churches are in a dilapidated state, with only a small amount of red painted wall decoration remaining.
