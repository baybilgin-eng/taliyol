Çınarlı Valley

Located within the boundaries of Mimarsinan Demokrasi Neighborhood in Kayseri Province, this valley opens at one end to the TOKI housing complex in the area, while the other opens to the park located on the northern border of Mimarsinan Organized Industrial Zone. The valley is called the "Plane Tree Valley" because it is home to hundreds of plane trees. The valley itself resembles a forest of plane trees. While you encounter dry fields when you exit the valley, you can walk among the plane tree forest when you descend into it. The valley contains numerous caves of varying sizes, some of which may have been used by humans in ancient times. A small stream carrying melting snow water flows through the valley during the spring months. It is a recommended valley for nature walks, enjoying the forest air, and exercising. The total length of the valley is approximately 8 km.
