Ahlat Seljuk Cemetery

Located along the main road in the Ahlat district of Bitlis, this cemetery contains numerous Seljuk tombs. Mausoleums can also be seen around the cemetery. There is also a museum on site displaying some of the artifacts unearthed from the cemetery. This cemetery, which has been meticulously renovated in a modern style, is definitely worth a visit.
