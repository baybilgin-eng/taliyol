Roasting Beko

We ate roasted meat at Kömürhan Kavurma, a place famous for its Beko Usta's roasted meats, located near the Karakaya Dam on the Elazığ-Malatya highway. Beko Usta offers an extremely clean and pleasant environment, and his roasted meat received full marks from us for its delicious taste. If you happen to be in the area, you should definitely try the roasted meat here; it's a family-friendly place with very reasonable prices.
