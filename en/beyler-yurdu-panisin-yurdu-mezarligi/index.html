Cemetery of the Lords' Residence (Panis' Residence)

Located behind Mount Kefeli, on the slopes of Mount Bohluca, this cemetery is situated in an east-west direction.

The identity of those buried in this plateau cemetery, located at an altitude of 2600 meters, and the date of its construction are unknown.

It's likely a Muslim cemetery, with around 100 graves of varying sizes.

A large tombstone has the date 1007 engraved on it, which corresponds to approximately the 1600s.

Looking down from the cemetery, the whole city seems to lie at your feet.
