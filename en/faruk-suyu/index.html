Lake Van Basin · 30 May 2013

Faruk Suyu

Located at the foot of Mount Erek, this mineral water spring has a rather interesting taste. With a metallic flavor and leaving a rust-colored residue wherever it flows, we can assume this water contains iron. This well-maintained spring emerges naturally from its surroundings.
