Aşık Veysel's Museum House

Aşık Veysel was born and lived in Sivrialan village, Şarkışla district, Sivas province. The village is located in a very beautiful setting surrounded by greenery.

Sivrialan village is the village where Aşık Veysel was born and lived, and the house where he lived in this village has been turned into a museum today.

The museum is equipped with state-of-the-art technology.

Zöhre Başer lives across from the museum house.

Zöhre is Aşık Veysel's eldest daughter.

While visiting their home, one should also kiss the hand of Mother Zöhre.

Exact Location:
