Karahöyük Mountain

This mountain, located east of Gürpınar (Salguma) village in Kayseri, has almost no vegetation (I think its name comes from this).

The mountain looks like a completely solid block of rock, and there are two large quarries on its northern side.

The mountain's summit is 1,777 meters high and there is a flag at the top.

The eastern side of the summit is flat, and there is a second peak immediately to the north.

The villagers believe the mountain is the village's water reservoir and are now complaining about the increasingly large quarries.

Erciyes offers fantastic photo opportunities from the mountain's summit.
