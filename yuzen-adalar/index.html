Lake Van Basin · 30 May 2013

Floating Islands

It is located in the Aksakal Göl hamlet of Hazarşah Village, Solhan district, Bingöl province. It is accessible via the Bingöl-Solhan highway. Currently, with support from the development agency, the surrounding area is being protected and developed as a recreational area. From what I saw, there are a total of 3 floating islands. The islands move freely on the water, and one of them even has two trees on it. While I had difficulty understanding what the islands were, I couldn't understand how the trees on them remained alive. I definitely recommend seeing the islands located in a magnificent natural setting. In the area where the islands are located, there are tables, seating areas, gazebos for picnicking, and even a children's playground.
