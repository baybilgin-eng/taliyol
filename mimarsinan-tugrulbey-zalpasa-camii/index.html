Mimarsinan Tugrulbey (Zalpasha) Mosque

Located on the Adilcevaz-Bitlis road, right on the shore of Lake Van, this mosque is called the Tuğrulbey (Zalpaşa) Mosque. An information sign on the mosque reads: “It is a 16th-century Ottoman work. It was built by the architect Sinan.” The mosque is in excellent condition and open for worship. A second mosque is located a little further up. The view of the lake from the mosque is breathtakingly beautiful.
