Melikgazi Mausoleum

Accessible via the Kayseri – Pınarbaşı highway, this tomb is extremely rare in terms of the materials it was constructed from, and it stands out among many tombs both architecturally and in terms of its location. The tomb is located within the boundaries of Melikgazi village. Built in the classic Seljuk dome architecture, the dome consists of two sections; the lower floor contains the burial chamber, and the upper floor contains the sarcophagi.
