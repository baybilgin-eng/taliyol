Milk Freezing Plateau

It's quite easy to guess where the name of this plateau, located in the high-altitude region of Mount Erciyes, comes from.

The plateau gets really quite cold at times.

The plateau is located right at the foot of Erciyes' glaciers, and the water flowing from the melting glaciers passes through the plateau and reaches the lower areas.

This plateau is also used by mountaineers as a campsite before summit climbs; in fact, there is even a mountain hut built for this purpose located here.

When I first saw this mountain house, I was amazed; I wondered how such a detailed mountain house could be built in such a difficult-to-reach place at such a high altitude.

There is a paved road that can be reached by jeep-type vehicles up to the plateau.

This is a plateau that anyone seeking clean air and temperatures of 15 degrees Celsius even during the hottest part of summer should definitely see.

From this plateau, Mount Erciyes offers such beautiful views that your eyes and soul will be delighted.
