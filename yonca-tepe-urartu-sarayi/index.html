Yonca Tepe Urartu Palace

Located west of the village of Yedi Kilise or Bakraçlı, this Urartian palace is approximately 10 km from the city center. Archaeological research and excavations have been carried out at the palace. Yonca Tepe Palace was built at a point overlooking the city center of Van.

Ancient grain and grape seeds have been found during excavations and research at the palace. Furthermore, the oldest known walking stick in history was also found here.

No Urartian cuneiform script has been found in the studies, leading researchers to believe that this palace may have belonged not to Urartu itself, but to an internal principality subject to Urartu.

The burial chambers are located on the northern slopes of the palace.
