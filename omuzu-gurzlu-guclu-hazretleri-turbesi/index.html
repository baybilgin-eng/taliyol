The Tomb of Omuzu Gürzlü (Güçlü) Hazretleri

Located on a high point north of the village of Sheikh Shaban (its old settlement), this tomb falls within the boundaries of the İncesu district.

The tomb is located on the Kızılören – Şeyh Şaban road, and a large mosque has been built right next to it.

The mausoleum also has a garden and a mosque.

It is said that large crowds visit and pray there during certain periods.
