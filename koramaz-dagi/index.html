Koramaz Mountain

This mountain, rising in the northeast of Kayseri, is located between Bünyan and Subaşı, and a TRT transmitter is situated at its summit.

On the western face of the mountain lies a freshwater spring known as Keşler Söğüdü.

There is a road leading all the way to the TRT transmitter on the mountain; you can reach it by car.

From the summit, the city looks amazing, and the altitude is 1,900 meters.

There are always at least two people at the transmitter station.

There is also a tumulus at the summit of the mountain, which has probably been looted previously.
