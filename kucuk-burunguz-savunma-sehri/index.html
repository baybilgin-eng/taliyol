Small Burunguz Defense Cave

Located within the boundaries of Küçük Bürüngüz village in Kayseri, this structure is also included in the Koramaz Valley inventory. The structure, which has numerous rooms and tunnels, features keystones (tırhız) at the entrances of the tunnels. There is also a three-arched room inside. Grain storage areas are located in the floors of the rooms, and a large part of the structure has collapsed. Numerous columbariums are also located near the structure.
